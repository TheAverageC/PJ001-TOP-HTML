For the coffe.

    Water
    2 tsp of grinded coffe of prefference per 250ml.
    1 tsp sugar or splenda or any sweetener if you like, not gonna judge.

Instructions...

For the coffe

  • Boil the water for like 3 mins.
  • while the water is boiling set a cup or something a bit bigger.
  • Put a filter over and pour the coffe, cuantity is as you wish or need.
  • Pour the water gently, not spoiling and let the water extract the coffe from the grinded grains.
  • When you finish you can add some sweetner if you wish, not gonna judge, once live gets bitter, yous tart enjoying the coffe with no sugar.
  • enjoy!
